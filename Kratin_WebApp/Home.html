Welcome to the Intelligent Medication Management System

About

The Intelligent Medication Management System is designed to simplify medication management and improve adherence. It offers a range of features to help you stay organized, receive timely reminders, and gain insights into your medication routine.

Key Features
  • Personalized Medication Schedule: Create and manage a customized medication schedule based on your prescriptions and dosage requirements.
  • Smart Reminders: Receive timely reminders to take your medications, ensuring you never miss a dose.
  • Medication History: Track your medication intake history, providing a comprehensive overview of your adherence.
  • Drug Interaction Warnings: Get notified about potential drug interactions and receive recommendations to prevent complications.
  • Refill Tracking: Keep track of your medication supply and receive reminders to refill your prescriptions in advance.
  • Health Insights: Gain valuable insights into your medication adherence patterns, empowering you to make informed decisions about your health.
